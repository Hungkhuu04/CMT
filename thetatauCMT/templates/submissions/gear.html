The Gear Article Form

A Gear article should be a 250-500 word article with pictures (and captions) about an event, major change, or area of growth in your chapter. It includes all of the fields below.
A Gear article is NOT an update on how many pledges / brothers your chapter has or on chapter officer changes, and it should not be confused with a newsletter. Newsletters are of interest to your chapter and chapter alumni. Gear articles are of interest to the national fraternity and alumni from other chapters.

Contributor AI Policy

With the rise of AI-assisted writing, we want to kindly remind contributors that The Gear has a single editor who generously volunteers their time to review every submission. We deeply appreciate that dedication and to honor it, please take care to submit only your most polished work. Quality over quantity makes a real difference and helps ensure great articles make it into The Gear in a timely manner.

  • You may use AI to brainstorm, outline, or edit.
  • You are responsible for everything submitted under your name.
  • Verify all facts, quotes, and sources.
  • Do not submit fabricated citations or copied content.
  • Add your own expertise, examples, and point of view.
  • Remove generic or repetitive AI-style writing before submitting.
  • Be prepared to explain how AI was used if asked.
